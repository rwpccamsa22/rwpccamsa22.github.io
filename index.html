COMPUTER APPLICATION AND BUSSINESS MANAGEMENT

The Department of Computer Application and Business Management offers a three-year full-time Diploma in Computer Application and Business Management (CABM) since 1999. This course is approved by the State Board of Technical Education and covers 50 students every year. This course is intended to provide insight into the theories and practical aspects of business management and the applications of computers in business. Students in a way that designs and implements the curriculum of the Diploma in Computer Application and Business Management:

  • S2 BATCH 2021-2024
  • S4 BATCH 2020-2023
  • S6 BATCH 2019-2022

This course is focusing on theories and practices related to business and other inter-disciplinary areas/ domains of management and applications of computers in business

VISSION

To create future women entrepreneurs and technocrats for nation building.


MISSION

1.To empower and develop women professionals by facilitating them to acquire managerial and technical skills.
2.To inculcate human values for future applications in industry and life.
3.To develop young women for global careers in industry and business, and to encourage them for higher studies.
