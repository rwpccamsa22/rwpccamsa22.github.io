WOMENS HOSTEL

It is the only Govt. polytechnic college for women in the state of Kerala with a “Residential” status. The college has comprehensive hostel facilities for the students. Seats are limited and allotted on first-come first-served basis. Hostel accommodation is available to a student, who is registered in the Institute as a regular student. Each student is provided with a table, a chair, and a cot. Good mess is provided to all the students. Mess is charged on the basis of dividing system.
