COMPUTER APPLICATION & BUSINESS MANAGEMENT

Department of CABM

Government Residential Women’s Polytechnic College, Payyannur is one among the five government polytechnics offering the course CABM, with an intake of 52 students. The course is entitled to impart both theoretical and practical knowledge of modern business to the students. This course provides insight to theories and practices on business and inter-disciplinary areas of management and applications of computers in business. Students from the department are able to handle computerized operations of all business activities, proficient in office automation necessary for business management, dynamic enough for handling and assisting all business operations including marketing, sales & customer care and well trained to assist office administration. Candidates for admission shall be required to have passed Xth class examination (S.S.L.C.) conducted by the Board of Public Examinations, Kerala State or any other equivalent examinations already recognized by the Board of Public Examinations, of Kerala State, with the eligibility for Higher Education.

There is a full-fledged computer lab and competent faculty to assist and guide the students. After completing Polytechnic diploma in Computer Application & Business Management, a student can apply for B. Tech in Computer Science & Engineering / Information Technology through lateral entry scheme. Students can also apply for private sector jobs as well as government sector jobs, as:
o Office Assistant
o Project Assistant
o Office Administrator
o Admin Executive
o Admin Assistant
o Personnel Assistant and Executive Assistant
o Asst. Graphic Designer
o Data Entry Operator
and more.

Vision & Mission

VISION

To create future women entrepreneurs and technocrats for nation building.

MISSION

  1. To empower and develop women professionals by facilitating them to acquire managerial and technical skills.
  2. To inculcate human values for future applications in industry and life.
  3. To develop young women for global careers in industry and business, and to encourage them for higher studies.

PEO, PO, PSO

PEO: Program Educational Objectives

  1.  To provide thorough knowledge in computer application and business management so as to adapt with ease to the changing environment.
  2.  To foster leadership and managerial skills with best professional ethical practices and social concern.
  3.  To possess a professional attitude as an individual or a team member with consideration for society and environment, and be able to communicate effectively, both orally and in writing.

Program Objective (PO)

  1.  Basic and discipline specific knowledge:- Apply the knowledge of basic management concepts and computing fundamentals to solve the problems in business organizations.
  2.  Problem Analysis:- Identify and analyze generic management and technological problems using structured methods and standards.
  3.  Design / development of solutions:- Develop alternative solutions for day to day problems of organizations and assist with design of system components or process to meet specified needs.
  4.  Information technology (IT) tools, Experimentation and testing:- Apply Information technology tools and appropriate techniques to conduct standard test to face the challenges of day to day operations of business organizations.
  5.  Technological and Management practices for society, sustainability and environment:- Apply appropriate management practices and technology in the context of society, sustainability, environment and ethical practices.
  6.  Project Management:- Use management principles individually, as a team member or a leader to manage projects and be able to communicate effectively.
  7.  Life-long learning:- Ability to analyze individual needs and engage in updating in the  context of technology and management.

PROGRAM SPECIFIC OUTCOMES ( PSO’S)

  1.  To acquire sound knowledge in the basic concepts of computer, management, business and accounting to deal with the varying complexities in the competitive era.
  2. To gain familiarity in the latest trends in various subjects of computer applications and develop innovative solutions.
  3. values, capable of accepting a vital role in various sectors of the society.

Facilities

Computer Application Laboratory

This lab is used to to facilitate the CABM students in their course curriculum . There are a total of 40 computers with internet connection and with state of art facilities. Lab courses conducted in this lab include:

 

TALLY- I

Students familiarize computerized accounting, the preparation of various statements of accounts. They are taught how to prepare inventory transactions and to understand the preparation of Bank Reconciliation and method of interest calculation.

 

COMPUTING FUNDAMENTALS

Students are introduced to the computer Fundamentals which includes both hardware and software. They familiarize with office automation tools which include the preparation of documents, spreadsheets, power point presentations, email creations and other office related works. They are taught basic programming concepts and also being introduced the programming language Python.

TALLY II

Students are familiarized with advanced inventory and the practice of calculating Value Added Tax. They are taught to practice service tax, TDS and TCS calculation, excise duty calculation and payroll creation.

 

OFFICE AUTOMATION SOFTWARE

Students are made to understand word processing and various related works. They are familiarized with spread sheet works. Students get equipped with presentation packages and the usage of database and related functions

 

RELATIONAL DATABASE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Students are familiarized with exercises to create and alter tables. They are taught about retrieval of information using functions. The lab covers exercises to understand queries. They are introduced to methods for creating users and setting privileges.

 

GRAPHIC DESIGNING

Students are made to practice Corel Draw, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Pagemaker ISM & type IT applications.

VISUAL PROGRAMMING

The lab aims to equip students with the knowledge and practice of arithmetic operations, string and matrix operations, form applications and web applications. This provides a platform for the students to demonstrate what theory they have learnt in visual programming paper.

 

WEB DESIGNING

The lab provides students with the platform to understand various browsers, to understand and use HTML, creates the ability to design web pages and to know about creating web pages using HTML.
